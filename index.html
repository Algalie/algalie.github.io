About Me

Combining academic excellence with practical experience

Building Digital Solutions That Matter

I am a Computer Programmer specializing in Software Engineering, with a strong foundation in building scalable, secure, and database-driven applications. I hold a Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Computer Science and have practical experience across full-stack web development, backend engineering, and system design. My technical skill set includes frontend technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; backend development using Python (Flask) and C#; and database management with Microsoft SQL Server, PostgreSQL, and Oracle. I have designed and implemented real-world academic and professional systems, including grading management systems, marketing management platforms, and enterprise-style CRUD applications featuring authentication and role-based access control. In parallel with application development, I am actively developing expertise in cloud infrastructure, system architecture, and cybersecurity fundamentals. I emphasize clean, maintainable code and software engineering best practices, with a focus on building systems that are reliable, secure, and scalable. I also maintain a strong interest in data-driven applications and the future integration of analytics and intelligent automation into modern software solutions. I am a disciplined, goal-oriented engineer with a strong problem-solving mindset and a commitment to continuous learning. My career objective is to grow as a professional software engineer while deepening my capabilities in cloud technologies, secure application development, and modern system design.
